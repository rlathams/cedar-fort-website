Core Message

The embedded governance intelligence and policy operationalization capability.

Stronghold Enforce™ is the included embedded governance intelligence and policy operationalization capability. It lets organizations begin with plain English, Word documents, PDF source material, or policy-as-code — then translate, review, validate, store, and operationalize that governance inside live workflows.

What Enforce Includes

Visible capabilities.

  • Plain English authoring
  • Word ingestion
  • PDF ingestion
  • Policy-as-code support
  • AI-mediated rule extraction
  • Review wizard
  • Syntax validation
  • Policy library and versioning
  • Regulatory linkage
  • Decision-engine integration
  • Core intelligence layer
