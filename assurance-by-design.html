The Framework

What Assurance by Design™ is.

Assurance by Design™ is Cedar Fort Digital's framework for embedding observation, traceability, proof, enforcement, and human accountability directly into the operational architecture of consequential decision systems.

Framework Principle

A structural property — not a supervisory layer.

Governance must be a structural property of the system, not a supervisory layer applied to it. That is the principle the framework rests on, and the basis on which the entire Stronghold™ suite is built.

Human Factor

Human judgment is a structural requirement.

Assurance by Design™ is not an autonomous-governance philosophy. Human judgment is built into the framework as a structural requirement — not added as an exception path.

Stronghold is built to elevate human judgment, not automate it away.

Role Model

Four named roles. Each given a workspace and a record.

Role 01

Signal Analyst™

Investigates signals. Builds workpapers. Documents evidence. Makes determinations on the front line of consequential work.

Role 02

Calibration Mentor™

Reviews determinations. Calibrates judgment across the team. Captures the patterns that make the practice stronger.

Role 03

Governance Architect™

Designs and tunes the governance environment: policies, methodology, audit universe, coverage intelligence.

Role 04

Assurance Authority™

The accountable executive view — sourced from governed records, never reconstructed.

Stronghold Vendor Sentinel™ adds a fifth named role — Vendor Governance Architect™ — when the module is licensed.

Important note

The framework was not built to meet standards checklists. It was built to solve the operating problem. Its broad standards alignment follows from sound design.
