speed of the operation.

A premium module for live vendor and third-party governance. It treats vendors as operational components, not questionnaire records — connecting telemetry, policy, dependency, evidence, and oversight in one governed workspace.

What it is today

Built for live vendor governance now.

Sentinel includes live telemetry, vendor tiering, governance policies, concentration risk, database-backed vendor records, vendor detail expansion showing signals, controls, and SLA context, hero metrics, search and filtering, sector-aware behavior, and Pattern 14 third-party AI risk visibility.

The model

From vendor oversight to dependency governance.

Sentinel is built around the idea that vendors are live operational dependencies. Every output is designed to be evidence-linked. Every risk view supports drill-down. Every feature treats vendors as active components of the governed environment — not static files or questionnaire records.

Where it's going

Expanding dependency-governance capabilities.

Why it matters

Govern the dependencies you depend on.
