The governance model is broken.

The Old Model

The Stronghold™ Difference

How It Works

From observation to sealed evidence in minutes.

A governance observation enters the system. Claude Sonnet 4.5 classifies it against 16 risk patterns in real time. The analyst opens a formal workpaper, documents evidence, and makes a determination. The record seals to the immutable vault instantly. The mentor reviews. The board report updates. No manual assembly. No evidence reconstruction.
