About Cedar Fort Digital

Why we exist

Built to make consequential work more governable, less painful, and more humane.

Cedar Fort Digital exists because governance failure is an architecture problem, not just a tooling problem. Stronghold was built to make consequential work more governable, less painful, and more humane.

Founder Story & Jacksonville Grounding

Built by people who have spent careers inside the institutions Stronghold protects.

The founder spent 25+ years at VP level inside Citigroup, JP Morgan, Freddie Mac, Fidelity, Wells Fargo, Florida Blue, and Amgen. The view from inside those institutions made the case for Stronghold clear: most governance failures are structural — and the way to fix them is structural.

Cedar Fort Digital is headquartered in Green Cove Springs, Clay County, Florida — outside Jacksonville. We serve regulated institutions worldwide.
